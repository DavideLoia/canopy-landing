Frequently asked
Is my data private and separated from other users?
Yes. Every Canopy account has its own isolated database. Your data is never shared with or visible to other accounts, teams or companies using the platform.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan at any time?
Absolutely. You can change your plan at any moment from your account settings. Upgrades are effective immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
How many users can I add to my account?
All paid plans are per-user. You pay for each active user you invite. You can mix roles (Admin, Editor, Viewer) across your team at no extra cost per role.
Do you offer a trial of paid plans?
The Starter plan is free forever and lets you explore the core platform. If you need a trial of a paid plan, contact us and we'll set one up for your team.
What languages and countries does Canopy support?
Canopy is available in Italian and English. It is designed for renewable energy developers operating in Italy and across Europe, with global expansion planned.
